Our Services

Our aim is to ensure you are provided with the best experience while travelling.

Delicacies

At Kenya Airways, we appreciate that our guests may have special meal requests that cater to dietary, religious or medical requirements. You can request your special meal 24 hours prior to your flight departure at through your travel agency, at any Kenya Airways sales office,

Flight Shoping

We offer international passengers new and exciting online, duty free shopping experiences, through our “Buy Before You Fly” service. This service entails that any passenger on an international flight can purchase duty free items at the time of booking a flight ticket, or up to 3 days prior to departures.

Shopping Catalogue

Airline in-flight duty free shopping catalogue: International airlines operate routes to many destinations and you will be able to buy goods from the on board selection. Child-safety
